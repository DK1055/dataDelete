Delete Your Account - Chill Manuals

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from our SuperPhotoScanner app:

  1. Contact us at: chillmanuals@gmail.com
  2. Include your account email (the one used to sign in).
  3. Your request will be processed within 7 business days.

Data Deleted:

  • Account credentials
  • Personal information
  • Associated user data

Data Retained:

  • None, unless required by law.

Retention Period:

Data is fully deleted from active systems within 7 days after account deletion request.
